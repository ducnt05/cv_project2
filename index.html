Personal Profile

A personal profile (or CV summary) is a 3–5 sentence introductory paragraph at the top of a resume that highlights your key skills, experience, and career goals. It acts as a hook to grab recruiters' attention, summarizing why you are qualified and what you can bring to the role.

Work Experience

Work experience encompasses paid jobs, internships, volunteering, and freelance projects, focusing on transferable skills and measurable achievements. Examples include using bullet points to show impact (e.g., "reduced, managed"), detailing quantifiable results like "increased sales by 15%," and incorporating tools/software.

Skill

Essential CV skills include a mix of soft skills (interpersonal) and hard skills (technical) tailored to the job description, such as communication, problem-solving, leadership, time management, and proficiency in tools like Microsoft Office or specific industry software. Highlighting these with concrete examples or metrics increases impact.
